Hi there, I'm Samiul Hoque!

Welcome to my homepage. Here I'll be blogging about everyday life, random projects, electronics, opensource hardware, cycling and maybe some pictures of my cat while we're at it.

I am a Maker/DIY Enthusiast and an avid follower of the maker movement and I take it as a life's mission to share the joy of making with everyone. I also happen to be a college dropout struggling with my startup, hoping to make it big someday.

Right now I'm at Ahmedabad, about 2500KMs away from home learning 'How to Make Almost Anything', the right way. It is a basically distributed model class taken by Neil Gerschenfield and a group of amazingly passionate makers at a network of nodes equipped with all the cutting edge tools. Hopefully, by the end of July I will be back in Bangladesh and start my own Fab Lab.
